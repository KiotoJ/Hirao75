Antso

I.A: Ry Tily ô ry Tily
     Andeha hiara-dia
     Ka aza mba mivily
     mandrakizay doria

1-Avia ry tanora
  hiarak'aminay avia
  Fa ady be tsy mora
  no hikatrohany Tily ô!

2-Izao tontolo izao
  no hikatrohanay avia
  tsy ady mora hatao
  Fa ady mahamay Tily ô!

3-Reseo ny hakamoana
  ny hakiviana koa. Avia
  fongory mba ho foana
  Ka mozotoa tokoa Tily ô!

4-Faingana ry tanora
  Andao hiarak'e! avia
  Hiarak'isan'ora
  Hanao dia rodobe TILY Ô!
